We're excited to announce the launch of Box, a new ERC-4626-compatible vault architecture we built from the ground up to rethink how DeFi vaults manage assets, allocations, and funding. So what exactly is Box? At its core, Box is a modular smart contract that serves as the foundation for a new generation of Steakhouse vaults. It holds an underlying asset like @withAUSD's AUSD or @SkyEcosystem's USDS, and from there it can allocate and deallocate liquidity across whitelisted tokens, pledge and depledge collateral, and manage borrowing and repayment through dedicated funding modules. Think of it as a single composable building block that handles the full lifecycle of vault operations, without the fragmentation you'd typically see across multiple contracts. But modularity alone isn't enough. We designed Box with safety at every layer. Every swap that happens inside a Box vault is bounded by oracle-based slippage checks, so no single trade can move outside of acceptable parameters. On top of that, cumulative slippage over time is also tracked and limited, providing a second line of defense against gradual value erosion. And if something ever goes seriously wrong, Box includes a permissionless shutdown mechanism, meaning anyone can trigger a winddown process that ensures depositors can always withdraw their funds, even in the worst-case scenario. These aren't optional add-ons. They're baked into the architecture from day one. Box also preserves the trust model that Steakhouse vaults have always been built on: 1. Timelocks for managing collateral whitelisting. 2. @AragonProject guardian veto rights. 3. Clearly defined escape hatches. The difference now is that all of this lives inside a cleaner, more flexible framework that lets us iterate faster and build more sophisticated strategies without sacrificing transparency or security. To mark this launch, we're kicking things off with the AUSD Term vault, which comes with 5% AUSD incentives. The Term vault represents a new way to access structured yield, purpose-built for users who want predictable returns without having to navigate the complexity that usually comes with DeFi yield strategies. Deposit, earn, and let the architecture do the heavy lifting. That was always the goal with this launch: simplify earning yield. DeFi has incredible infrastructure under the hood, but too often that complexity gets passed directly to the end user. With Box powering our vaults, we can abstract away the moving parts while still giving users full transparency into what's happening with their capital. Simple on the surface, robust underneath. And this is just the beginning. Box is designed to be a foundation we build on, expect more vaults, more strategies, and more assets powered by this architecture in the months ahead.
